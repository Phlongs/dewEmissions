What We Offer

DEW Emission testing facility offers one of the most convenient locations for Gwinnett and Forsyth drivers. The facility is conveniently located in Sugar Hill, on the corner of Cumming Highway (20) and Suwanee Dam Road. The test takes less than 10 minutes and you can stay in your car while we work.

DEW Emissions is the closest testing facility to the Gwinnett and Forsyth border, making it convenient for residents of Sugar Hill, Buford, Suwanee and Cumming. (It’s OK for you to get tested at our Sugar Hill, Gwinnett facility, even if you live in Forsyth County.)
